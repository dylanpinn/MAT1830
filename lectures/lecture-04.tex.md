## 4.1 Connectives ∧, ∨ and ¬

Define $\land$ by the following _truth table_:

| $p$ | $q$ | $p \land q$ |
|-----|-----|-------------|
|  T  |  T  |     T       |
|  T  |  F  |     F       |
|  F  |  T  |     F       |
|  F  |  F  |     F       |

Define $\lor$ by the _truth table_:

| $p$ | $q$ | $p \lor q$ |
|-----|-----|------------|
|  T  |  T  |     T      |
|  T  |  F  |     T      |
|  F  |  T  |     T      |
|  F  |  F  |     F      |

Define $\neg$ by the _truth table_:

| $p$ | $\neg p$ |
|-----|----------|
|  T  |  F       |
|  F  |  T       |

### Notation

$$\land \text { "and" }$$
$$\lor \text { "or" (inclusive) }$$
$$\neg \text { "not" }$$
